Number of volunteers: 100/250

Volunteering Info

Looking for individuals who can comfortably pick up trash in the sun for 6 hours. We are also looking for approximately 6 individuals who can haul trash to the Paradise Landfill throughout the day. (Costs will be covered by us) Volunteers will be rewarded with free pizza at 7pm on the day. Meet with Lidnsay at the Mahi Mahi Shack near the parking lot for sign in and

Commitments

Other than the time commitment, volunteering for this event requires that you swear off our enemies, the Cleanup Rivals. This group has for some reason been devoted to “dirtying down” the places we clean up. Luckily, we are not expecting any deviance from them at this event, so new signups will NOT be required to attend the Cleanup Rivals Defense Training (CRDT), which is ordinarily offered before our events.
